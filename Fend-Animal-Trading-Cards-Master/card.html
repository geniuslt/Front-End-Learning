Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers make good family pets, particularly as they are patient with children

  • Scientific Name: Golden Retriever
  • Color: gold or cream
  • Life span: 10-12 years

The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl such as ducks and upland game birds during hunting and shooting parties,[3] and were named 'retriever' because of their ability to retrieve shot game undamaged.
